The AI industry is moving fast in Washington. To take on Big Tech and ensure AI benefits rather than harms everyday Americans, we need to work together.

Prompt Action exists to bring together the humans who care, including:

  • Privacy advocates furious about AI-powered mass surveillance
  • Environmentalists furious about illegal gas turbines poisoning children in Memphis
  • Parents furious about chatbots encouraging their kids to kill themselves
  • Women furious about Grok still making sexualized images of them
  • Workers furious about displacement with no transition plan
  • National security people furious about chips being sold to adversaries
  • Anti-war activists furious about Minab
